This website has the goal of assisting you in the process of making sound investment decisions in the stock market. In order to accomplish this goal we focus on:

  • Basic concepts that everyone should know before making an investment.

  • Providing the tools necessary to analyze the fundamentals of any public company.

You will be able to create a portfolio of public companies that you are interested in. In order to do so, you will have to insert data available in the anual reports of each company.

If you do not want to do the research, you have the option to access the data by entering the ticker symbol of the company you are interested in. Just press on the following link.

Weighing which option is better for you will depend on whether you trust a third party to run the numbers for you or if you are a do it yourself person.

In order to get the data directly of each company we use Yahoo finance API.
